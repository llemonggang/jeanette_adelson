Projects

Zodia

Zodia is an app to check your daily horoscope. Using an API to pull in your daily, love, wellness, and career horoscopes, it's a fun way to stay up-to-date with the cosmos. There's also information about the moon phases, and sunrise and sunset times, based on your current location. The App uses fun icons, reflected on an Astronomy Picture of the Day, straight from NASA. Future plans include adding information about planets and how they affect your horoscope, information about how different zodiac signs generally relate to each other, and exciting news related to the cosmos!

Urban Libs

UrbanLibs is your childhood, but adult-style: Madlibs meets Urban Dictionary. A group project that generates a random sentence, then random words from urbandictionary.com and their definitions. Made with React, it's a single page application that is fun when you're not having fun. Pull it out at parties and let the silliness prevail!
