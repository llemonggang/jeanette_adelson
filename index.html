Projects

  • Zodia

    Zodia is an app to check your daily horoscope. Using an API to pull in your daily, love, wellness, and career horoscopes, it's a fun way to stay up-to-date with the cosmos. There's also information about the moon phases, sunrise, and sunset times based on your current location. The App uses fun icons, reflected on an Astronomy Picture of the Day, straight from NASA. Future plans include adding information about planets and how they affect your horoscope, information about how different zodiac signs generally relate to each other, and exciting news related to the cosmos!

    • Urban Libs

      UrbanLibs is your childhood, but adult-style: Madlibs meets Urban Dictionary. A group project that generates a random sentence, then random words from urbandictionary.com and their definitions. Made with React, it's a single page application that is fun when you're not having fun. Pull it out at parties and let the silliness prevail! Collaborators can be viewed on Github.

Our focus was to produce the minimum viable product to then build on later. The focus of the site is to connect people who wish to donate food, with people who need food for their own services. Fellow developers and thier work can be viewed on Github.

    • Tic-Tac-Toe

      Tic-Tac-Toe was my first challenge with Javascript, and a huge lessons in writing functions, as well as learning to conceptulaize an entire project before beginning to code. Using a basic html template, the guidlines were as follows:

      • When the page loads, Player 1 (X) is the first player to go
      • When Player 1 clicks an empty cell, it should be marked with an "X", and Current Player should switch to two
      • When Player 2 clicks an empty cell, it should be marked with an "O", and Current Player should switch back to one
      • If a player clicks a cell that has already been marked, they should see an alert letting them know that the cell in question has already been taken
